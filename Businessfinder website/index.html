About

We understand the power of networking and making the right connections. Working in collaboration with both Mossel Bay and George Business Chambers, as well as hosting our own networking meetings in Mossel Bay and George on a weekly basis. The Business Finder is a business directory with a twist, offering you a mini website within our website, as well as world-class digital marketing at an affordable rate. In addition to your own website, The Business Finder listing will also show on the first page of Google Results, along with all your social media listings, increasing the possibility of a potential client clicking on your details for their needs. We have various other offerings available to suit your marketing needs. Alongside digital marketing, we also believe in personal connections.
